Advance search

Find a site with... To do this in the search box.
all these words: Enter key words: terrier calico mouse
this exact word or phrase: Put the exact word in quotation marks: "rat terrier"
any of these words: Type OR between all the words you want: minimize OR standard
none of these words: Put a minus sign right before the word you don't want: -rodent, -"Jack Russell"
numbers in the range: next Place 2 dots between numbers and add units of measure: 10..35 lb, $300..$500, 2010..2011
Then narrow down your
results by...
language: Find the page in the language of your choice.
region: Find pages published in a specific region.
Most recent update: Find the page that is updated during the time you specify.
website or domain name Search on a website (like wikipedia.org ) or limit your results to a domain like .edu , .org or .gov
where the word appears: Search for terms throughout the page, page title or web address, or link to the page you're looking for.
Safe search: Tell SafeSearch whether to filter pornography.
file type: Find the page with the format you want.
right to use: Find the site that you are authorized to use.
